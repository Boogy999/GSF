iMentor

Connecting students with industry professionals for personalized mentorship and career guidance.

Program Overview

iMentor is our flagship mentorship program that pairs students with experienced professionals in their field of interest. Through one-on-one guidance, workshops, and networking opportunities, we help students develop the skills and connections they need to succeed.

How It Works

Application

Students apply through our online platform, sharing their interests and goals.

Matching

We carefully match students with mentors based on interests and career goals.

Regular Sessions

Weekly one-on-one sessions with mentors for personalized guidance.

Community

Access to workshops, networking events, and a supportive community.
